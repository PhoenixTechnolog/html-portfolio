My Top 3 MOvies Of All Time

The Platform

This movoie showcases the greed that rich or powerful individuials have. This is a movie prisoners who are fed on a decending platform. Those on the upper levels take more than their share and thus the lowest levels receive nothing. It is a deep psychological thriller.

Cloudy With A Chance Of Meatballs

This is a cartoon. Its silly but shows a real life perspective that if foof can be manufactured in seconds and fell from the sky, most of us would only eat junk food.

Die Hard

I think this is a severely underrated action movie. In my opinion this is the best Christmas movie without all the jazz of an unrealistic world showcasing a happy ever after. It has some comedy but its full action.
